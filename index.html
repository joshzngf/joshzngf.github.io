JPTT

JPTT is an app that makes going onto PTT, the biggest forum in Taiwan, easier. The original PTT is an old telnet BBS, which requires users to use a terminal emulator to browse through it. With JPTT, users can just use the app with intuitive touch interface and browse with ease. Also, JPTT provides functions that the original PTT does not offer, such as adding articles to collections, or getting notified when someone respond to the article you followed. The backend system of this app, which allows user data backup is powered by Parse, and the article recommendation system is powered by Node.js. JPTT currently has more then 50,000 users and has a 4.3 stars of rating on Google Play, and it's also available on the App Store starting from Sep. 2015.
